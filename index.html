What to add next

The missing pieces that would make VCReady much stronger.

You already have strong modules. What’s missing now is the product glue — the layers that make everything feel connected, premium and worth coming back to.

Founder report export

This is a real must-have. After completing modules, founders should be able to export a polished report summarizing readiness, weak points, benchmarks and priority actions.

  • One-click PDF summary
  • Shareable with co-founders or advisors
  • Turns the platform into a decision tool, not just a calculator

Readiness roadmap

Founders should not leave with just scores. They should leave with a ranked action plan showing what to fix first and what will move investor perception the most.

  • Priority actions by impact
  • 30 / 60 / 90-day improvement logic
  • Makes VCReady habit-forming

Account layer + saved progress

Right now the site is useful. With accounts, it becomes a platform. Saved progress, history and benchmarking over time create retention and product seriousness.

  • Persistent founder workspace
  • Progress history over time
  • Better entry point for paid plans later

Investor-facing mode

Long term, one powerful angle is letting founders generate a cleaner investor-ready snapshot. Not a data room replacement — a high-level readiness memo.

  • Clean summary of the company profile
  • Signals maturity and discipline
  • Extends the product beyond internal use

About

Built to close the gap between operating a company and fundraising it well.

VCReady came from a simple observation: too many founders enter fundraising conversations without a precise view of how investors will read the company. The problem is rarely effort. It is usually structure, interpretation, and timing.

Why it exists

Founders often know fragments of the story — a few metrics, a rough valuation view, a decent deck — but not the full readiness picture. VCReady is built to make that picture legible.

  • Founder-friendly, but investor-aware
  • Practical, not academic
  • Built around fundraising reality
